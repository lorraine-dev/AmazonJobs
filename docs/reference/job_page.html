DESCRIPTION

Amazon is looking for an analytical and technically skilled Senior Business Intelligence Engineer (BIE) to join our 3P Selection team. This role requires an individual with strong analytical abilities, solid business acumen, and comfort with technical systems. The successful candidate will be a self-starter comfortable with ambiguity, with strong attention to detail, and an ability to work in a fast-paced environment. This position can be based in our Tech Hubs in Luxembourg, London, Paris, Madrid, or Milan. Amazon will support your relocation. Come build the future with us!

Key job responsibilities
• Partner with the 3P Selection team to leverage techniques from econometrics, computer science, and statistics to measure seller experience and its impact on selection growth
• Build and maintain data pipelines and analytics solutions to track selection metrics across EU marketplaces
• Create innovative dashboards and reporting tools to support selection growth strategies and catalog gap analysis
• Collaborate with Product Managers and Data Scientists to develop selection guidance tools and frameworks
• Support strategic decision-making through advanced analytics and data-driven insights
• Drive automation and improvement of selection reporting mechanisms
• Partner with cross-functional teams to analyze selection trends and opportunities

A day in the life
• Work with large-scale datasets to analyze selection patterns and identify growth opportunities
• Develop and maintain dashboards tracking key selection metrics across EU marketplaces
• Support the implementation of AI/ML solutions for selection optimization
• Create automated reporting solutions for tracking selection growth and catalog gaps
• Partner with stakeholders to translate business requirements into technical solutions
• Provide data-driven insights to support strategic selection initiatives

About the team
The 3P Selection team is responsible for driving Amazon's EU marketplace selection strategy, managing a catalog of 2.3B items. You'll work alongside Product Managers, Data Scientists, and other BIEs to develop and implement solutions that enhance selection growth and improve the seller experience across EU marketplaces.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

- Experience programming to extract, transform and clean large (multi-TB) data sets
- Experience with theory and practice of design of experiments and statistical analysis of results
- Experience with AWS technologies
- Experience in scripting for automation (e.g. Python) and advanced SQL skills.
- Experience with theory and practice of information retrieval, data science, machine learning and data mining
- Experience working directly with business stakeholders to translate between data and business needs
- Experience with SQL
- Experience with data visualization using Tableau, Quicksight, or similar tools
- Experience in the data/BI space

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

- Experience managing, analyzing and communicating results to senior leadership
